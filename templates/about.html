About Us

CampusConnect Bot is designed to assist students by providing quick answers to common queries. Built using Flask, NLP techniques, and Logistic Regression, it efficiently recognizes intent and delivers accurate responses.

This project highlights the power of natural language processing (NLP) and demonstrates how web-based chatbots can improve accessibility, streamline communication, and enhance student engagement.
